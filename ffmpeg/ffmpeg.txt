
如何编译ffplay

1.安装sdl2
sudo apt-get install libsdl2-dev



2.开启--enable-sdl
./configure --enable-shared --enable-sdl --enable-gpl --enable-nonfree --disable-stripping --enable-zlib --enable-avresample --enable-decoder=png --enable-pic --disable-symver --extra-cflags="-fPIC"

说明:
--extra-cflags="-fPIC" 64位机器上启用动态库符号链接
